Transportation

To get the full San Francisco experience you should take the BART once, and use a convenient ride sharing service like Uber or Lyft. There will be parking at the Log Cabin (the wedding location) but that’s about it. Pay attention to street cleaning signs and driveways to avoid getting towed.

Use 511.org to map your route using public transit
511.org

Use BART to get downtown or across the bay.
bart.gov

Use Uber or Lyft to get home when you can’t find a cab or when you need a reliable service to take you to the airport, but make sure you sign up for the service and install the phone app before you try to use it.
